An Adventure In The Dark

You are an adventurer, lost in a dark forest. You try to look around to find any landmarks, when you spot an old church in the distance, towering over the crooked trees. As you hear the boom of a lightning strike in the distance, you decide that the best course of action would be to seek shelter in the church. As you enter the church, you realise that it has been abandoned for a significant amount of time as you can see there are cracks in the floor and all the furniture has started to fall apart. The only exception this is the two doors to the left and right of a staircase that leads down into a void of blackness. The left door is made of mahogany and has a red glow to it and the right door is made of ebony wood with a blue glow to it. The staircase is made of black coloured granite, and the black void that is at the bottom of it fills you with dread.
